What Is Astrology?

Astrology is based on the idea that the position of heavenly bodies at the time of a person's birth can influence their personality, destiny, and future events. This practice, thousands of years old, sees a connection between the cosmos and earthly life, suggesting that celestial movements and positions can reflect or influence the rhythms of our daily existence.

The Meaning of the Sun Sign in Astrology

Sun Sign In
Astrology?

Your Sun sign is determined by the position of the Sun in the zodiac at the time of your birth. It represents your core identity, your ego, and the fundamental qualities that drive your sense of self. When people casually refer to their "star sign," they are usually referring to their Sun sign.

The Sun moves through each zodiac sign for approximately one month per year, creating twelve distinct solar personality archetypes. While your Sun sign is only one piece of your full astrological profile, it forms a critical foundation for understanding your essential nature and life purpose.

Astrology is a vast and intricate system. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced sky-watcher, there's always more to discover about yourself through the cosmos.

Your Sun sign is determined by the position of the Sun at the time and date of your birth. Each sign spans roughly 30 days of the year. If you were born on a cusp day (the transition between two signs), your exact birth time and location are needed to determine your precise Sun sign using a birth chart calculator.
Western astrology uses the Tropical zodiac, which is aligned with the seasons and the Earth's relationship to the Sun. Vedic (or Jyotish) astrology uses the Sidereal zodiac, based on the actual positions of the constellations. This means your Sun sign may differ by one sign between the two systems.
Astrology doesn't predict the future in fixed terms; rather, it reveals cycles, tendencies, and energetic themes that may influence your experience. It is a tool for self-awareness and timing—helping you understand the best moments to act, rest, reflect, or initiate change.
A birth chart (or natal chart) is a map of the sky at the exact moment of your birth. It includes the positions of the Sun, Moon, and all planets in the zodiac signs and houses, along with the aspects (angles) they form to each other. It is your personal cosmic blueprint.
Many people find an annual reading valuable for reviewing the major astrological themes of the upcoming year. Others seek readings during significant life transitions or when navigating challenging periods. Solar return readings (around your birthday) are also a popular yearly check-in.
