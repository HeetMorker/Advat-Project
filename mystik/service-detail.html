Home/Services/Matrix Energetics

Matrix Energetics is a complete system of healing and transformation that uses the principles of quantum physics, subtle energy, and the power of consciousness to create profound, lasting change.

Developed by Dr. Richard Bartlett, Matrix Energetics is not a technique so much as a new way of perceiving reality. It works with the body's innate intelligence and uses two-point and other methods to collapse old patterns and introduce new possibilities at a quantum level.

Unlike conventional energy healing that focuses on moving or manipulating energy, Matrix Energetics teaches you to access a wave-like state of consciousness where anything is possible — where pain, limitation, and illness can dissolve and be replaced by new, expanded states of being.

What to Expect in a Session

During a Matrix Energetics session, you'll be asked to stand or lie down comfortably. The practitioner will make light contact (or work remotely) and use a combination of intention, consciousness focus, and the two-point technique to create shifts in your field.

Many people experience spontaneous physical sensations such as warmth, tingling, a sense of wave-like movement, or a deep sense of relaxation and peace. Some experience emotional releases or sudden insights. Others notice changes in their physical body that occur gently and surprisingly quickly.

Sessions typically last 60 minutes and can address:

  • Chronic pain, injury, or physical illness
  • Emotional trauma, anxiety, and depression
  • Limiting beliefs and mental patterns
  • Relationship and life transition challenges
  • Spiritual development and awakening
  • Stress, burnout, and exhaustion
